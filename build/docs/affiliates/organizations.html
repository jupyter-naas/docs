Organization Affiliates

NCOR partners with organizations across sectors to advance the development and implementation of ontologies.

CUBRC

Independent not-for-profit R&D company specializing in information fusion, data science, and ontology development.

Raytheon Technologies

Aerospace and defense company partnering with NCOR on ontology-based system integration projects.

Science Applications International Corporation

Technology integrator delivering IT and technical services solutions.

KadSci

Consulting company specializing in developing data-driven AI capabilities

NaasAI

Universal Data & AI Platform connecting organizational data with AI using ontologies.

TRADOC Operational Environment Center

Supporting the US Army with operational environment analysis and frameworks.

Improvado

Marketing data aggregation platform working on ontology-based data integration.
